**Welcome to Leon's Space Website!**
This website has been meticulously developed by our team to cater to the needs of our esteemed client. - Multiple User Logins: Enjoy the convenience of accessing the website as an admin, customer, or staff member, each with tailored privileges and functionalities.

- Index: Welcome to our website's homepage, where you can discover featured products and announcements.
- Contact: Reach out to us effortlessly through our dedicated contact page.
- About: Learn more about Leon's Space and our mission.
- Product: Browse our extensive collection of products with ease.
- Single Product: Dive deep into the details of individual products for a comprehensive shopping experience.
- Blog: Stay informed and entertained with our engaging blog posts.
- Login/Register: Seamlessly access your account or create a new one to unlock exclusive features.
- FAQ: Find answers to commonly asked questions for a smooth shopping experience.
- Checkout: Streamline your purchasing process with our intuitive checkout page.
- Single Post: Delve into specific blog posts for in-depth reading.
- Staff Dashboard: Empower your staff with access to order details and management functionalities.
- Customer Dashboard: Personalize your shopping experience and manage your account effortlessly.
- Edit Customer Details: Update your customer information securely and conveniently.
